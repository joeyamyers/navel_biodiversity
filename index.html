Project Background: Researchers are interested in bacteria that synthesize proteins that taste like beef. They have teamed up with the start-up, Improbable Beef, to research candidate species that will provide the perfect taste. Researchers have hypothesized the microorganism that can provide the best taste commonly resides in the human belly button. Each volunteer's identity is anonymous and have been assigned an ID number.
